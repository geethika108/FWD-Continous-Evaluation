Smart Billing System 

    The growth of the retail industry has created a strong need for efficient and reliable store management systems. 
    Traditional billing and inventory processes often involve manual operations that can lead to errors, data loss, and inefficiency. 
    The Smart Management System aims automate these tasks through a computerized platform that integrates billing, inventory management, and sales tracking. 
    By providing real-time updates and organized data handling, the system ensures accuracy, transparency, and improved customer service within retail environments.

    Features of the Smart Billing System:
    1. Automated Billing: Generates accurate bills quickly, reducing human error.
    2. Inventory Management: Tracks stock levels in real-time, alerting for low inventory.
    3. Sales Reporting: Provides detailed sales reports for better business insights.
    4. User-Friendly Interface: Easy to navigate for both staff and management.
    5. Data Security: Ensures that all transaction data is securely stored and backed up.
